 Features:
 1. No Motion(palm)
 2. Cursor Movement(V-Gesture)
 3. Left Click(Index down Middle Finger up)
 4. Right Click(Index up Middle Finger Down)
 5. Scroll Up,Down(Left hand pinch move up,down)
 6. Multi-Selection(Right hand grab)
 7. Double click(Join Index,Middle fingers together)
 8. Drag-drop objects(Index,Middle fingers down)
 9. Zoom in-out(Join index,Middle fingers)
 10. Brightness Control(Right Hand pinch UP,Down)
 11. Sound Control(Right Hand pinch Slide left,right)
